About Us

Welcome to Active Heaven, where passion meets fitness and sports! We are a group of dedicated students from Assumption University in Thailand, working together to create a platform that makes renting sports fields and gym memberships seamless and accessible.

Our story began as part of a database project for our coursework, but we envisioned something more. We wanted to build a system that not only showcased our technical skills but also brought value to our community by simplifying the booking process for sports enthusiasts.

Whether you're looking for a soccer field for your next friendly match or a gym to stay in shape, Active Heaven has you covered. With our user-friendly platform, you can book your favorite facilities in just a few clicks.
